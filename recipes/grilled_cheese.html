Grilled Cheese

A beautifully made grilled cheese.

This recipe isn't like your ordinary grilled cheese. It has basil.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Get two pieces of bread
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  3. Place cheese and basil on the buttered side of either slice
  4. Put both pieces of bread together, buttered side on the inside
  5. Grill the sandwich for 3 minutes on each side
  6. Plate and serve!
