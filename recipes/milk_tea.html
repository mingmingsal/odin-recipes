Milk Tea

A sweet milk tea.

This recipe isn't like your ordinary milk tea. It has milk.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Tea Bag
  • Water
  • Ice
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil water for 10 minutes
  2. Stop boiling and Place tea bag for 3 minutes
  3. After 3 minutes, remove tea bag
  4. Add sugar and stir
  5. Add ice and milk. Stir.
  6. Add in cup and serve!
