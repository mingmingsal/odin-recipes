Congee

A delectable congee.

This recipe isn't like your ordinary congee. It has meat.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Bouillon Cube

Steps

  1. Boil water for 10 minutes
  2. Place rice and boil for 30 minutes
  3. Add meat and boil for an additional 10 minutes
  4. Add Bouillon cube for flavor
  5. Quickly stir
  6. Plate and serve!
